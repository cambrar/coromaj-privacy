Privacy Policy for Flower Drawing

Last Updated: February 2026

Developer: Coromaj

This Privacy Policy describes how Coromaj ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in relation to the Flower Drawing mobile application ("App").

Children's Privacy

Flower Drawing is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13 years old. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable laws protecting children's privacy.

We do not collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Our App does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos or media (except what you create and save locally)
  • Contact information
  • Any data that could identify a user

How the App Works

  • Drawing Creation: All drawings are created and stored locally on your device only
  • Save Feature: When you save a drawing, it is saved to your device's local storage/gallery
  • Share Feature: When you choose to share a drawing, your device's standard sharing function is used. We do not have access to or store any shared content
  • No Internet Required: The App functions entirely offline. No data is sent to external servers
  • No Accounts: The App does not require or support user accounts or login

Third-Party Services

Flower Drawing does not integrate any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Data Storage

All data created within the App (your drawings) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the App, locally stored drawings remain in your device's gallery unless you delete them.

Parental Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to review, delete, or modify. Parents can uninstall the App at any time to remove it from their child's device.

Advertising

Flower Drawing does not contain any advertisements.

In-App Purchases

Flower Drawing does not contain any in-app purchases.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
